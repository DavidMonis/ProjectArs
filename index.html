Co-op strategy + adaptive AI teammates

Vision

Build a new story-driven cooperative strategy game where you play alongside friends or an adaptive AI teammate that can seamlessly substitute a missing player and gradually learn your playstyle.

Year 1 — Foundations

  • Game: simple strategy loop (build → gather → train units → final battle).
  • Leaderboard: lightweight board shared on the website.
  • AI: learn the basics; build a first PyTorch agent; join a beginner Kaggle competition.
  • Devlog: weekly YouTube updates; public GitHub; AI notes on the web.

Year 2 — Integration

  • Connect the AI agent with the custom game.
  • Design a more capable agent tailored to the strategy gameplay.
  • Draft the concept & narrative skeleton of a simple multiplayer story game.
  • Continue outreach; add Shorts (YT/IG/TikTok), maybe Patreon.

Year 3 — Expansion

  • Build the planned simple story-strategy multiplayer title.
  • Deepen AI specialization; start next-year’s applied agent.
  • Enhance community: Discord/Forum, polish the pipeline.

Year 4 — Application

  • Deliver an AI agent that can play the new game well and ship it.
  • Evaluate commercial potential and sustainability.

Weekly Rhythm (Year 1)

Alternate focus between Game Engines (1, 2) and Thesis Project (1, 2). Post weekly progress to YouTube and the thesis page, then publish a combined update to the ProjectArs website.
